Adult Population in Indian States

There are presently 28 states in India. The total population of the country is 1.21 billion presently. According to the State Census 2011, the most populated state in India is Maharashtra. The least populated state in the country is Sikkim with a population of 60,7688.

Its also evident that Northern States of the country have high male population rate. Southern and North eastern states have high female population rate.
